# TOGAF Class: Governance Log

The **Governance Log** serves as the official registry of all architecture governance activity, board decision histories, active waivers, and project compliance reviews.

---

## 📂 Governance Log Subdirectories

The governance log is structured into three areas to track accountability and compliance:

### 🗳️ 1. Board Decisions
*   **Path**: `decisions/`
*   **Contents**: Meeting minutes of the Architecture Review Board, formal approval logs for Statement of Architecture Work, and strategic tech evaluations.

### 📜 2. Waiver Registry
*   **Path**: `waivers/`
*   **Contents**: Active and archived waiver requests. Each file follows the `WAV-YYYY-XXXX.md` format, tracking standard deviations, risk analysis, and progress on remediation plans.

### 🔍 3. Compliance Reviews
*   **Path**: `reviews/`
*   **Contents**: Formally executed Architecture Compliance Assessments conducted at Stage-Gate reviews (Design Sign-off, Pre-Deployment Staging Audit, and post-production SLA evaluations).
