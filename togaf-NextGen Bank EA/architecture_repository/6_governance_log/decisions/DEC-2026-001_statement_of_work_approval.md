## 2. Context & Problem Statement

To initiate Phase A (Architecture Vision) of the Micro-Loan project, the Architecture Board must formally approve the Statement of Architecture Work (SOW). The SOW outlines:
*   Project boundaries (in-scope vs. out-of-scope).
*   RACI responsibility matrix for deliverables.
*   Workplan milestones and stage-gate reviews.

---

## 3. Decision Outcome

The Architecture Board has **formally approved** the SOW as written in [statement_of_architecture_work.md](file:///Users/manavshrivastava/Documents/github/untitled%20folder/togaf/architecture_repository/3_architecture_landscape/segment/digital_lending_stp/statement_of_architecture_work.md).

### Rationale:
*   **Decoupled Scope**: The SOW successfully insulates NextGen Bank's legacy Core Banking System (CBS) by establishing a dedicated DLU Lending Ledger, minimizing regression risks.
*   **Clear Stakeholder Alignment**: Power-interest grids and communication channels are appropriately mapped, mitigating cross-department blockage.
*   **Stage-Gates Definition**: Governance gates are aligned with the bi-weekly agile development cycle.
