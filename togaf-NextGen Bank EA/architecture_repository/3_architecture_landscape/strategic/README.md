# Landscape Tier: Strategic Architectures

Strategic Architectures provide a high-level, long-term view of the entire enterprise's technology and business landscape. They establish the strategic direction, target states, and timelines for the overall bank's digital transformations.

## Contents
This folder acts as a placeholder for enterprise-wide architectural blueprints, such as:
- **Enterprise Target Operating Model (TOM)**
- **5-Year Corporate Cloud Transition Plan**
- **Bank-wide Data Sovereignty Blueprints**

## Usage Guidelines
Strategic architectures are referenced during the initialization of new product segments to ensure that segment boundaries align with broader corporate policies.
